China Automotive Hiring Comparison

Audience: HR leadership Language: English Date: July 2026

Executive Summary

This report compares hiring activity for two major Tier‑1 automotive suppliers in China — Bosch and Continental — based on a small sample of open roles collected in July 2026. Both companies are actively recruiting for software-defined vehicle capabilities, with roles spanning ADAS perception engineering and embedded systems development. Market context signals confirm sustained investment in software talent across China's automotive sector.

Key findings: The sample covers 2 roles across 2 companies in 2 cities (Shanghai and Suzhou). AUTOSAR is the only skill appearing in both job postings, suggesting it remains a common requirement for automotive embedded and ADAS roles in China. Hiring is concentrated in the Yangtze River Delta, with Bosch recruiting in Shanghai and Continental in Suzhou — consistent with the region's established automotive supply chain.

This report is based on a limited sample of 2 job postings and 3 market-context sources. All figures are illustrative; broader data collection is needed for statistically meaningful conclusions.
