Executive Summary

Market Context

Automotive companies in China continue investing in software-defined vehicle capabilities and talent. Market intelligence indicates sustained demand for embedded software and ADAS engineering roles across major Tier-1 suppliers. The sample analyzed is limited but points to consistent competition for software engineering talent in the region.

Key Finding — Hiring Activity

Two active job postings were identified across two Tier-1 suppliers (Bosch and Continental), both targeting embedded/ADAS software roles in Eastern China. Both companies are hiring for roles that require AUTOSAR expertise, signaling shared demand for standardized automotive software architecture skills.

Key Finding — Geographic Concentration

The observed postings are concentrated in Shanghai and Suzhou — two established automotive R&D hubs in the Yangtze River Delta. This aligns with broader industry patterns where Tier-1 suppliers cluster engineering teams near OEM customers and automotive technology parks.

Note: This report is based on a limited sample of 2 job records and 3 market context references. Findings are directional and should be validated against broader market data before making strategic workforce decisions.
